## About me

I build and operate scalable backend, cloud, and real-time networking systems for online games.

Currently, I work as **CTO at DreamRain Co.**, where I lead technical strategy, infrastructure architecture, backend development, real-time game networking, DevOps workflows, and live-ops systems for production games.

My work sits at the intersection of **multiplayer game technology**, **server-side architecture**, **Unity ecosystem tooling**, **self-hosted infrastructure**, and **AI-assisted moderation**.

- **Unity engineering**: SDKs, plugins, IL2CPP Android builds, asset delivery, build pipelines, and live-update workflows
- **Data & AI systems**: Redis, SQL/NoSQL databases, analytics pipelines, LLM/Ollama-based internal tools, and Persian chat moderation research
